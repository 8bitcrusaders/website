Welcome To Our 8-Bit World!

What We Can Bring To Your Next Event

The 8-Bit Crusaders is a duo from The Metro-Detroit area that consists of two nerds who want to bring Retro Video Gaming & Video Game Culture to events around The Midwest and beyond. You can see photos of our events, our logos, posters, etc. below and more information about our videos on youtube.

Odd-Ball Games & Odd-Ball Systems:

Erik & Rudy like to do Reviews/Playthroughs and skits of games that are a little strange or something most people would pass up to show how they are and the fun to be had with them.

Is It Up or Is It Down?:

The 8-Bit Crusaders like to do reviews of games with an un-biased look and instead of giving a rating system or something like that they either give a thumbs up, thumbs down, or just an in-between. They spice up the content with skits involving the game and more to keep their viewers interested. 

Everyday Gems:

Everyday Gems is a segment with Rudy & Erik discussing common games for many systems that actually are pretty awesome! Including their distinctive skits and more to show you cool ways to appreciate these retro games that you may remember. 

Ultimate Retro Game Lounge:

Take a step back in time with the Ultimate Retro Game lounge including decor, retro CRT televisions, game systems from almost any era depending on request, and so much more. 

Live Deejaying:

Erik & Rudy provide the soundtrack for you to game by! They both produce House & Techno music among other electronic styles, so they love to bring that sound to the events that they set up at. 

Prizes & Merchandise for Sale:

The 8-Bit Crusaders have game competitions with awesome prizes varying on type and they also have custom merch along with used and new video games/accessories. 

Original Content:

Erik & Rudy produce original songs for release and mixtapes with original music and non-original music for your events to get attendees excited. 

Live Performances:

The 8-Bit Crusaders hit the stage with high energy and want to get your event going! Performing on CDj's, can add live instruments, and more depending on the event. 

Many Years of Experience: 

Erik & Rudy have both been deejaying for 10+ years a piece both performing on many stages across the midwest and beyond alongside some big names in the music industry.
